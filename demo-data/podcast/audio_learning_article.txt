Title: AI Podcasts for Learning

Section 1: Benefits
AI-generated podcasts can make dense material easier to consume by turning documents into conversational explanations.

Section 2: Limitations
Podcasts can oversimplify technical material if the system does not preserve important details.

Section 3: Cognitive Load
A good learning podcast should reduce cognitive load but still provide enough structure for review.

Section 4: Personalization
The best podcast experience adapts to the learner's weak topics, preferred pace, and recent study history.

Section 5: Source Grounding
Podcast claims should be grounded in the uploaded document so users can trust what they hear.

Section 6: Quiz Follow-up
After listening, users should receive quiz questions to confirm understanding.
