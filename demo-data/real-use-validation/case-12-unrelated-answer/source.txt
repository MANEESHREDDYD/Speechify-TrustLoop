Title: Urban Garden Watering Guide

Section 1: Morning Watering
Water vegetable beds early in the morning so leaves can dry during the day. Apply water near the soil rather than spraying the leaves.

Section 2: Frequency
New seedlings may need light daily watering during hot weather. Established plants should be watered less often but more deeply.

Section 3: Mulch
A five-centimeter layer of straw or leaf mulch reduces evaporation and limits weeds. Keep mulch a small distance away from plant stems.

Section 4: Rain
Skip scheduled watering when recent rain has left the soil moist several centimeters below the surface.
