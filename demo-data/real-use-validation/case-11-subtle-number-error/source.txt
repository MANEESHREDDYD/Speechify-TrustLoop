Title: Library Usability Test Results

Section 1: Participants
The usability study included twelve participants: six undergraduate students, four graduate students, and two faculty members.

Section 2: Task Completion
Ten participants completed the book-renewal task without help. Eight completed the room-booking task without help.

Section 3: Main Finding
The most common problem was locating the room-booking link on small screens. Five participants first opened the events calendar instead.

Section 4: Recommendation
The team recommends placing a room-booking button on the mobile home screen and testing the revised label with another small group.
