# Account Export Requirements

Account owners request an archive from privacy settings, and preparation may take up to twenty-four hours.

The archive contains profile details, uploaded files, activity history, and consent records but excludes internal fraud-review notes.

Links expire after forty-eight hours, require another sign-in, and the stored archive is encrypted.

Marcus owns the interface design and confirmation messages. Elena owns archive generation, encryption, and link expiration.

Security testing and accessibility review are required before launch.
