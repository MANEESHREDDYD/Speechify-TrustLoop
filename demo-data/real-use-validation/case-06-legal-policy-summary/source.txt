Title: Volunteer Data Handling Policy

Section 1: Collection
Volunteers may collect a participant's name, email address, and attendance choice only when the information is needed to organize an event.

Section 2: Access
Only the event coordinator and the designated privacy lead may download the participant list. Volunteers may view the list during check-in but may not copy it to a personal device.

Section 3: Retention
Participant lists must be deleted within thirty days after the event unless a participant separately agrees to receive future updates.

Section 4: Incident Reporting
A lost device, mistaken email, or suspected unauthorized access must be reported to the privacy lead within twenty-four hours.

Section 5: Appeals
A volunteer whose access is suspended may request a review from the program director within ten business days.
