Title: Community College Course Participation Policy

Section 1: Attendance
Students may miss up to three class meetings without a grade penalty. A fourth unexcused absence lowers the participation grade by five percentage points. Documented medical or caregiving emergencies may be reviewed by the instructor.

Section 2: Late Work
Assignments submitted within forty-eight hours of the deadline receive a ten percent deduction. Work more than forty-eight hours late is accepted only when the instructor approves an extension before submission.

Section 3: Accessibility
Students who need disability accommodations should contact the accessibility office. Approved accommodations may include extended test time, captioned media, or alternative document formats.

Section 4: Privacy
Class recordings may be used only by enrolled students for personal study. Recordings may not be posted publicly or shared outside the course.
