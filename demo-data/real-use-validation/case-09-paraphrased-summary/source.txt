Title: Repair Cafe Operating Guide

Section 1: Intake
Visitors describe the broken item at the welcome desk. A volunteer records the item category and asks whether the visitor has already attempted a repair.

Section 2: Safety
Only trained volunteers may open devices connected to household electricity. Batteries that are swollen, leaking, or unusually hot must be isolated and taken to the safety table.

Section 3: Repair Process
The visitor stays with the item while a volunteer diagnoses it. The program teaches repair skills rather than accepting items for unattended service.

Section 4: Parts
Common donated parts are free. If a special replacement part is needed, the visitor decides whether to purchase it and remains responsible for the cost.

Section 5: Records
The cafe records the item category, whether the repair succeeded, and the estimated weight diverted from waste. It does not record the visitor's home address.
