# Repair Cafe Overview

At arrival, guests explain the faulty object and mention any fixes they have already tried.

Electrical appliances can be dismantled only by qualified helpers, while dangerous batteries are moved to a dedicated safety area.

Owners participate throughout diagnosis because the service is educational rather than a drop-off repair shop.

Routine donated components cost nothing, but visitors pay for unusual replacement parts they choose to order.

The program tracks repair outcomes and waste reduction without collecting residential addresses.
