Title: Small Business Cash Planning Note

Section 1: Current Position
The business has forty-eight thousand dollars in available cash. Average monthly operating expenses are sixteen thousand dollars, so current cash covers about three months before considering new revenue.

Section 2: Concentration Risk
One customer represents forty-two percent of monthly revenue. The contract renews in November, and the customer has not yet confirmed renewal.

Section 3: Receivables
Twenty-seven thousand dollars of invoices are more than thirty days old. Management expects some payment this month but has not committed to a collection date.

Section 4: Planned Spending
The proposed equipment purchase costs twelve thousand dollars. The note recommends delaying the purchase until the major contract renewal and overdue invoices are clearer.

This planning note is informational and is not investment, accounting, or legal advice.
